Our Team

Delchi Fafach, P.E.
Project Manager

Delchi grew up on a small farm in Boulder County. His early experiences with engineering, starting at age 5 or so, included elaborate tree forts, deep tunneling excavations, and other mechanical inventions. As a high school student, he enrolled in the Building Trades program at Front Range Community College, now Boulder TEC. As a boy, he pursued service opportunities and part-time work in construction locally and in Mexico.

He enrolled in the Architectural Engineering program at CU Boulder to pursue his interest in the building industry, where he discovered a delightful interest in formal engineering sciences and applied mathematics. After graduation with a minor in math, he helped build a log cabin in Israel and then began structural engineering in Boulder. He eventually returned to CU for a master’s of science in structural engineering.

Delchi lives with his family in Lafayette, CO. His passion is to use structural engineering skills and a knowledge of construction to help other people every day. See more information about Delchi on Linked In at

Jonathan Wallace, P.E., S.E.
Project Manager

Jonathan has many years of experience in the design of residential and commercial structures in the Boulder/Denver region. Over the last decade, he has developed a reputation with local architects and builders for his successful management of complex, fast paced, and budget sensitive projects. He joined DLK in 2011 as a project manager with full control over his own projects, from proposal to design and construction. He maintains his own growing base of clients who have sought his involvement in their projects time and time again.

Jonathan is also passionate about serving others, both locally and worldwide. This passion has led him to live in Thailand to help start a non-profit organization to underprivileged teens, mentor local college students, and to provide engineering services for development organizations such as Habitat for Humanity, TSC Global, and Engineering Ministries International. He enjoys exploring the outdoors and spending time with his growing family. See more information about Jonathan on Linked In at

Daniel Belleau, E.I.
Structural Engineer

Daniel is a Colorado native, born in Boulder, and then spending the vast majority of his childhood in Fort Collins. His excitement for engineering and construction started at a young age, where he spent many summers learning the masonry trade from his grandfather. His interests were further reinforced in college when Daniel spent three weeks in Thailand rebuilding homes after the devastating tsunami of 2004.

Daniel attended the University of Colorado, where he graduated Summa Cum Laude with a degree in Architectural Engineering. He began his career as a structural analysis engineer and project manager for a Boulder-based company specializing in civil structure testing. He spent five years traveling around the US and internationally testing various civil structures and installing long-term structural monitoring systems.

Daniel and his wife, and their two dogs, live in Erie, CO. Daniel’s personal passions are skiing, backpacking, hunting, and mountain biking. His professional goal is to leverage his knowledge and skills with diligence and integrity in order to better serve those around him. See more information about Daniel on LinkedIn at
